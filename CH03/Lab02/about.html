About Us

Wild Rescues is a regustered non-profit wildlife rescue and rehabilitation facility, located in Ocala, Florida. We help injured, sick, neglected, and orphaned wildlife. We help rehabilitate these animals and then release them back into the wild, if possible.

Wild Rescues is a member of the National Wildlife Rehadilitators Association.

We help many animals, such as:
  • Raccoons
  • Squirrels
  • Fox
  • Birds
  • Horses
  • Deer
  • Pigs
  • Reptiles

Did you find an inhured or orphaned animal? Contact us to see if we can help.
